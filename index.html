OUR LATEST SERVICE

WORD

  • Speaker: Laurah
  • June, 22 2025

SERVING GOD
SERVE GOD
SERVING GOD
TEXT: GEN 26:1-14, DEUT 28:1-15


The ultimate example of service is found in our Lord, Jesus Christ. He, who was God incarnate, did not come to be served, but to serve. Mark 10:45 is incredibly powerful: "For even the Son of Man did not come to be served, but to serve, and to give his life as a ransom for many." Jesus didn't just teach about service; He lived it. He healed the sick, fed the hungry, comforted the sorrowful, and ultimately gave His life on the cross. His service wasn't glamorous; it was gritty, sacrificial, and utterly selfless. He calls us to follow in His footsteps. If the King of Kings came to serve, how much more should we, His followers, embrace a spirit of service? ...
