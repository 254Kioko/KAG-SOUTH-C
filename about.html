Welcome to KENYA ASSEMBLIES OF GOD, SOUTH C, a community centered on faith, love, and service. Our journey began in 1984 when a group of passionate individuals came together with a vision to create a place of worship and fellowship.

Our Community

KAG SOUTH C CHURCH is more than a place of worship; it's a vibrant community of individuals and families dedicated to living out their faith. From engaging Sunday services to midweek gatherings, youth programs, and outreach initiatives, we offer a variety of opportunities for fellowship, learning, and service.

Our Values

Rooted in the teachings of Christ, our values guide everything we do. We believe in:

  1. Inclusivity: Embracing diversity and welcoming everyone regardless of background or beliefs.
  2. Community: Building strong relationships and supporting one another in our spiritual journeys.
  3. Service: Engaging in acts of kindness, generosity, and service to those in need.
  4. Faith Formation: Providing opportunities for learning, growth, and deepening our understanding of God's word.

Get Involved

Whether you're new to South C or searching for a spiritual home, we invite you to join us. Explore our services, connect with our community, and discover ways to get involved. Together, let's grow in faith and make a positive impact in our world.

Our ministries stand as vibrant avenues for engagement, growth, and community. Whether you're seeking spiritual enrichment, ways to serve others, or simply looking to connect with like-hearted individuals, we have diverse ministries tailored to meet various needs. From uplifting worship experiences and educational programs to outreach initiatives that extend a helping hand to those in need, there's a place for you to contribute your unique gifts and find fulfillment in making a difference .

Come, explore our ministries, discover where your passion aligns with our missions, and together, let's embark on a meaningful journey of faith and service."

Contact Us

We'd love to hear from you! If you have any questions or want to know more about KAG SOUTH C CHURCH, feel free to reach out to us through our contact page or visit us during our service hours. We're here to welcome, support, and journey alongside you in your spiritual walk. Thank you for being part of ourKAG SOUTH C CHURCH family!.
